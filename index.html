What Kind of Thinker Are You?

Character 1: Thinker

Description: A thinker is a person who does a lot of thinking. This person may be very intellectual, a great conversationalist, but a really big procrastinator. It may take this person a while before they finally come up with a rational decision. They use logic, consistency, and weigh the pros and cons before diving head first into decision making.

Character 2: Feeler

Description: A feeler will constantly make their decisions based on how others around them will feel by their actions. They are very empathetic and caring. When it comes to discussions, feelings will think of themselves as the subject at hand and will speak on the topic as they feel the topic would want to be spoken of. They are very focused and innovative people.

Character 3: Sensor

Description: Sensors are people who are going to make sense of a situation and its possible outcome before making the decision. They think back upon their past experiences before making irrational decisions. They almost always find the best solution for themselves.

Character 4: Intuitive

Description: The intuitive decision-maker never neglects the gut feeling they have when decision making. They’re very in tune with their emotions and their higher selves. They know what is and what isn’t for them. They notice patterns easily and know how to go about situations that they’ve foreseen as negative.
